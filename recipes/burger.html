Burger

Description

This gourmet burger is a celebration of flavor and texture, featuring a succulent, hand-formed patty that's been seasoned and grilled to juicy perfection. Nestled between a toasted brioche bun, the beef's natural richness is complemented by the smooth, velvety melt of aged cheese. Crisp lettuce adds a refreshing crunch, while atop the patty, a generous helping of caramelized onions introduces a sweet and savory depth. Each layer of this burger builds upon the last to create a symphony of taste, culminating in a dish that's as indulgent as it is satisfying. Ingredients

  • Ground beef patty
  • Brioche bun
  • Aged cheese slice
  • Lettuce leaf
  • FCaramelized red onions

Steps

  1. Shape ground beef into patties and season with salt and pepper.
  2. Cook patties on a hot grill to desired doneness.
  3. Lightly toast brioche buns on the grill.
  4. Place a slice of cheese on each patty during the last minute of grilling to melt.
  5. Place lettuce on the bottom bun, add the cheese-topped patty, and finish with caramelized onions.
  6. Cap with the top bun and serve immediately.
